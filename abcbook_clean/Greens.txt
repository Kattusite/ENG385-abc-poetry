A
A stands for Apple, which here you may see.

B
B is for Ball, as round as can be.

C
C begins Cat, and Carpet, and Cup.

D
D is for Dash, who nicely sits up.

E
E is for Elephant, monstrous and high.

F
F begins Fox, and we know he is sly.

G
G stands for Gander, for Goose, and for Gift.

H
H begins Horse, whose paces are swift.

I
I is an Infant, and dressed out in silk.

J
J is a Jug, to hold water or milk.

K
K is a Kitten, that plays with a ball.

L
L is a Ladder, for climbing a wall.

M
M is a Mouse, that nibbles the cheese.

N
N stands for Nuts, that grow on the trees.

O
O stands for Owl, that eats mice for its prey.

P
P stands for Parrots, some green and some grey.

Q
Q stands for Quills, from the goose or the swan.

R
R stands for Rabbits, for Fanny or John.

S
S stands for Snail, with its house on its back.

T
T stands for Tiger, striped yellow and black.

U
U stands for Urn, to hold water for tea.

V
V is a Violet, which here you may see.

W
W is a Windmill, that turns with the wind.

X
X is a letter like this X, you will find.

Y
Y is a Youth, who has many kind friends.

Z
Z comes the last, and here it all ends.
