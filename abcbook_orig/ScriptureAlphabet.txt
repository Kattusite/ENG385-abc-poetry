THE SCRIPTURE ALPHABET
FOR
CHILDREN

LONDON
JOHN HARRIS
CORNER OF ST.PAUL'S CHURCH-YARD

As the first impressions that are formed on the infant mind are found to be the most permanent, It is the deisgn of this little work by means of Graphical Illustrations accompanying each Letter of the Alphabet to impress on the earliest dawning of reason clear &amp; distinct ideas of the principle characteristics of the sacred scriptures.
