ABC 's of FOREST FIRE PREVENTION

H
H IS FOR HEALTH
And HAPPINESS, too.

I
I IS FOR INDUSTRY
(Commerce and trade)
With the buzzing of saws
Much lumber is made.

J
J IS FOR JACK
The lumberjack bold
Who cuts the big logs
In the forests so old.

K
K IS FOR KEEN
Which children should be
In guarding the land
Of the green Maple Tree.

L
L IS FOR LUMBER
That's shipped overseas.
They couldn't build houses
If it weren't for the trees.

M
M IS FOR MAPLE
From which we get sap.
If killed by a fire
We'll have no trees to tap.

N
N IS FOR NOTICE
Put up by the ranger
To warn every camper
Of forest fire danger.

O
O IS FOR OUT
As a camp-fire should be
For if it's left burning
There's danger, you see.

P
P 'S FOR PROTECTION
While our forest grows.
So the pulp from, the pulpmills
Can make paper and clothes.

Q
Q IS FOR QUICKNESS
When fire is found,
Quenching it quickly
Saves the forest around.



R
R IS FOR RANGER
Stalwart and true,
His eye's on the forest
He's working for you.

S
S IS FOR SERVICE
That each child can give
To help prevent fire
So our forests may live.

T
T IS FOR TREES
Nature's great gift to man
Let us guard and preserve them
As well as we can.

U
U IS FOR USEFUL
A fine thing to be
You can be useful
By planting a tree.

V
V IS FOR VALUE
In health, wealth and beauty.
They are found in the forest;
To protect them's our duty.

W
W 's FOR WATCHFULNESS
All should display,
For fires may break out
By night or by day.

X
X IS FOR XMAS
That comes once a year.
Its tree is a symbol
That children hold dear.

Y
Y IS FOR YIELD
That the forests will bring
Year after year,
A permanent thing.

Z
Z IS FOR ZEAL
And also for zest
To prevent forest fires.
Let us all do our best!



A
A IS FOR ALERT
Which children should be
To report any fire
They may happen to see.



B
B IS FOR BONFIRE
That dances and leaps
If allowed to run wild
Awful havoc it reaps.

C
C IS FOR CAMPER
Thoughtful and good,
Careful with fire
When near a wood.

D
D STANDS TOR DEATH,
DESTRUCTION and DANGER
"Be careful with, fire"
Says the Forest Fire-Ranger.

E
E IS FOR EMBER
That is "too small to burn."
It may wipe out a forest
Before you return.

F
F IS FOR FIRE
"Good Servant, bad Master;"
If not "kept in its place"
It burns faster and faster.

G
G IS FOR GAME
The forest's its home.
When the forest is burned
It has no place to roam.
